What exactly is a Software Developer?

As a software developer you will have certian requirements, such as:

  • Designing
  • Programing
  • Building
  • Deploying and Maintaining Software
  • Help Build Software Systems

What They Do Exactly

As a Software Developer For a Tech Company Such as Appleor Tesla and You Could Be Required To:

  1. Design software applications
  2. Develop performance metrics or standards related to information technology
  3. Develop testing routines or procedures
  4. Document technical specifications or requirements
  5. Modify software programs to improve performance
  6. Supervise information technology personnel

All Requirements

Apple's Requirements:
- BS or MS degree in Computer Science or Computer Engineering plus 3-5 years of software development experience.
Tesla's Requirements:
- Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or similar areas of study. Minimum 2 years’ experience in Mobile (iOS/Android) development.
- Familiar with JavaScript. Experience with React/React Native is preferred.
- Excellent grasp of fundamental computer science concepts, good at solving complex technical problem.
- Experience using common design patterns. High standards for code quality, maintainability, and performance.
- Most Other Requirements:
- Bachelor Degree:
- 4 Years Minimum
- Either in, Software Engineering, Computer Science, or in a similar field.
- Some Senior Levels May Require a Masters Degree
- Certifications:
- Certified Associate in Software Quality
- Master ACE (Advanced Certified Engineer)
- Certified Network Systems Technician
- Certified Manager of Software Testing
